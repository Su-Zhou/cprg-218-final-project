About Us Page

Get to know us on this page. Our brand, our goals, our founding team and what we believe in all right here!

Our Brand

We want to deliver premium quality stationery and art materials to local residents in the city who just want an aesthetic collection for their creative needs.

Our Goals

To inspire you to be excited to journal, write or do anything creative that comes to your head! Here at Akari Stationery, it all started for our small love of journaling, crafts and art. This may the start of your creative journey.

Our Team

  • Akari Mizunaki

    "I grew up in a small town in rural Japan before moving here to Canada. I've always liked journaling so together with my two best friends, we created Akari Stationery!"

  • Micaelah Kari

    "I met Akari when we were both freshmen at university. Just like her, I've always been a craft and creative person. I wanted to help her dream and my dream come to life with this stationery brand."

  • Sera Ryubicki

    "I actually met both Micaelah and Akari when they visited my art exhibition for my final art school project. We immediately all became friends and the rest was history with now the creation of Akari Stationery that continues to flourish day by day."
